    • üî£ EMI Formula Used

    The EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is calculated using the standard formula:

    EMI = [P x R x (1+R)N - 1]

    Where:

  • P = Principal loan amount
  • R = Monthly interest rate (annual rate / 12 / 100)
  • N = Loan duration in months
